Process

How UITS Works

1
Register & Get TIN
Citizens and businesses register online. A unique Taxpayer Identification Number is instantly generated and linked to all your records.
2
Automatic Data Sync
Your TIN links to banks, Companies Registry, Motor Traffic, Land Registry, and Customs. Financial activity is recorded in real-time.
3
AI Calculation
The Tax Calculation Engine automatically computes your liability — income tax, VAT, excise, customs — with zero manual entry required.
4
Pay Online
Pay via bank transfer, mobile wallet, card, or auto-debit. Get instant confirmation and government receipts. No queues, no paperwork.
